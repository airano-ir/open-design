Collaborative approvals should begin as a reversible workflow layer

Enterprise teams need accountable sign-off without turning Open Design into a heavyweight governance suite.

RecommendationBuild a native two-step approval layer behind a workspace flag; defer policy engines and external approval integrations until usage proves the need.

The current handoff loses accountability at the exact moment risk rises

Teams export work for review in chat or email. The artifact, feedback, and final decision then live in different systems, leaving creators unable to prove who approved what.

31%of enterprise trials mention approval or sign-off
2.4 daysmedian delay between final edit and acceptance
18%of review comments repeat resolved feedback
